## SIG Mission and Scope

SIG-Space aims to build a high-reliability, strong real-time, and intelligent full-scenario collaborative open-source ecosystem for space computing based on openEuler. It covers aerospace (satellite constellations, deep space exploration) and low-altitude (UAV, eVTOL) domains.

- Why create a new SIG in openEuler: The rapid growth of commercial aerospace and low-altitude economy demands a unified, open-source operating system foundation for space devices. Current space-borne and airborne software systems are vertically closed, deeply hardware-coupled, and suffer from fragmented ecosystems, which hinders rapid innovation.

- The scope of the SIG: Space-grade OS baseline definition, high-reliability system hardening (SEU protection, power-loss resilience), real-time kernel optimization (PREEMPT_RT, mixed-criticality), hardware abstraction layer & BSP for heterogeneous platforms, embedded AI enablement, and space protocol middleware (CCSDS, DDS).

- Which SIGs in openEuler to cooperate with: Embedded SIG (hardware abstraction), AI SIG (inference framework optimization), Kernel SIG (real-time kernel patches).
